Flexicalymene Fossile Trilobite

Flexicalymene, a species of trilobite come from the Ktaoua Formation in Morocco North Africa and date back to 450 million years ago of the Paleozoic Era, Ordovician Period finally became extinct at the end of the Permian Period, about 250 million years ago.

Cube

Geologists use trilobites in a variety of ways to help them understand how the Earth has developed. One use is in the relative dating and stratigraphical correlation of sedimentary rock successions and also to reconstructing past geographies (palaeogeography) and past environments (palaeoenvironments)
